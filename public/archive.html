The Holy Orphanage of Blah and Baby stands as a solemn reminder of the commitment of faith and love, amidst a world of difficulty and heartache. Founded long ago, it was a place of refuge for those in need, and a place of comfort for those whose hearts had been broken. The orange walls of the orphanage stand in testament to the hope that can still be found in difficult times, and the reminder that no one is ever truly alone. The children here have been raised with love, compassion, and a commitment to the highest of values. Despite their difficult beginnings, these children have gone on to lead successful and fulfilling lives, and the legacy of this orphanage will live on for generations to come.

The Monastery of Automelodious Bears

Created By

Ginna Lambert

Auto. Music. Bears.

The Monastery of Automelodious Bears stands in silent reverence, a testament to the ancient and mysterious power of music and the spiritual solace of these majestic creatures. Here, in the tranquil hills, monks of all ages and backgrounds come together to meditate upon the divine harmony of these two forces. The peaceful melodies of the monks’ instruments intermingle with the gentle song of the bears, creating a harmony that is felt throughout the entire valley. The monks believe that the music of the bears is a gift from the gods, a reminder of the beauty of nature and the importance of protecting it. Here, the monks and the bears coexist in perfect harmony, and all who visit the monastery are reminded of the power of music and the majesty of bears.

Crystal. Castle. Jesus.

A majestic castle looms in the distance, its walls and towers gleaming in the sunlight with a mysterious hue. The castle has been standing for centuries, a testament to the faith and devotion of its inhabitants. It is the Crystal Castle of Jesus, a holy site dedicated to the teachings of the Lord. The walls are adorned with intricate carvings and mosaics depicting the stories of Jesus and his works. Inside the castle, one finds a place of peace and solace, a place of prayer and worship. The Crystal Castle of Jesus stands in grandeur, a reminder of all that Jesus stood for and all that he accomplished.

The Temple of the Divine Canine

Created By

Momin Khan

Super cute doggo. My life as a teenage robot. 15 pound serrano ham.

The Temple of the Divine Canine stands as a monument to honor the beloved canine companions of the world. It's ornate architecture glimmers in the sunlight, with depictions of man and his furry friends carved into its walls. Inside, a great golden statue of a beloved pooch is enshrined, surrounded by a bright, shimmering altar. Pilgrims from all corners of the world come to worship and meditate on their own canine companions, paying homage to the 15 pound serrano ham that sustains them. Here, they can remember the joys of their life as a teenage robot, the love and loyalty of the super cute doggo that fills their hearts.

Hall of Proud Farts

Created By

Josh Kugler

Thou must fart proudly. Thou shalt not shuffle thy truffle. Thou shalt read many goosebumps books.

The Hall of Proud Farts stands tall in the very heart of the city, a monument to the spirit of those who have refused to be silenced. It is a place of pilgrimage for many who come from far and wide to pay tribute to the courage of those who refused to shuffle their truffle. Inside, one finds a library of Goosebumps books, a reminder that great things can be accomplished when we are unafraid to stand up and fight for what we believe in. It is a solemn and inspiring place, a reminder that we can all be proud of our actions no matter how small they may seem.
